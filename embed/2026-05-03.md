# A new chess variant; etc.
## May 3, 2025

I discuss a new chess variant, then discuss some lies 
manosphere men believe.

### d20Capa

*d20Capa* is a chess variant based on some of the research I have been
doing with chess variants.  In this variant, I have taken 20 of the most
balanced Capablanca chess setups, and have the opening setup randomly
chosen before the game starts with a 20-sided die.

[Its full rules are in the linked file](d20Capa.html).

outlier](https://archive.ph/20251222152226/https://nuancepill.substack.com/p/are-men-uglier-than-women).
