Analysis

1. e4 e5 2. Nf3 Nf6 3. Nxe5 Nxe4?!

As my chess mentor put it, after 3... Nxe4?!, you have made one mistake. Don’t make another mistake...and 4... f6? was the second and decisive mistake.

4. Qe2 f6?

5. Qh5+ g6 6. Nxg6 hxg6 7. Qxg6

Not 7. Qxh8? because we want to keep the attack on the king going.
